# Quiz Generator Skill

This skill allows you to generate quizzes based on provided content. You can specify the topic, difficulty, and number of questions.

## Usage

To use this skill, provide the text or topic you want to create a quiz from, and optionally specify the desired difficulty and number of questions.

**Example:**

User: "Generate a 3-question quiz on the American Civil War, easy difficulty."

Assistant: (Invokes Quiz Generator skill)

Skill Output:
```
Quiz: American Civil War (Easy)
1. Who was the President of the United States during the Civil War?
   a) George Washington
   b) Abraham Lincoln
   c) Thomas Jefferson
   d) Ulysses S. Grant

2. What was the main cause of the American Civil War?
   a) Taxation without representation
   b) Slavery
   c) Religious freedom
   d) Economic depression

3. Which battle is considered a major turning point in the Civil War?
   a) Battle of Gettysburg
   c) Battle of Antietam
   d) Battle of Saratoga
```
